    Why OpenStreetMap is not known more widely?

    People are often using and appreciating OpenStreetMap data without being aware at all about its source.

    OpenStreetMap license requires giving a clearly visibly credit.

    We should start treating this requirement seriously, as ongoing plagiarism is harmful to OpenStreetMap project.
